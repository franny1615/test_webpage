This is a webpage with Emperor Penguin pictures.
These magnificent creatures of the South Pole(Antartica) can grow up to be upwards of 3Ft.
They can live up to 20 years of age, and can weight up to 45kg(about 100lbs).
Their diet consist of "fish, crustaceans and cephalopods."
In other words fishies, shrimp, and octupus.
